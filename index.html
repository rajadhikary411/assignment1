Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"


Innovation

"Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on innovations like smart agriculture, renewable energy solutions, AI in healthcare, and digital education to drive sustainable development."

Sustainability

Bangladesh is a rapidly growing nation with a rich cultural heritage, vibrant economy, and strong focus on sustainability, technology, and development.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure is rapidly developing, with investments in transportation, energy, communication, and urban planning to support economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment involves providing individuals with the tools, opportunities, and confidence to make decisions, improve their lives, and contribute positively.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation refers to integrating digital technologies into all aspects of business and society, enhancing efficiency, innovation, and connectivity.

Green Energy

Green energy refers to energy derived from renewable, environmentally friendly sources like solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal, aiming to reduce carbon emissions

Modern Insfrastructure

Modern infrastructure includes advanced systems for transportation, energy, communication, and public services, designed to support economic growth and sustainability.

Bangladesh is a South Asian country known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. Located on the Bay of Bengal, it boasts lush greenery, rivers, and a vibrant history. The nation gained independence in 1971 and has since grown in various sectors, including agriculture, textiles, and technology. The Sundarbans, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger, and Cox’s Bazar, the longest sea beach in the world, are major tourist attractions. Despite challenges like population density and climate vulnerability, Bangladesh continues to progress with resilience and innovation, fostering a sense of unity and pride among its people.

Recent News

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a prosperous and sustainable future driven by technology, innovation, and modern infrastructure. It reflects a digital and green Bangladesh where education, healthcare, and employment are accessible to all citizens. Smart urbanization, renewable energy, and the integration of technology in agriculture and industries will elevate the economy to new heights. Special focus will be placed on women’s empowerment, equitable development, and addressing the challenges of climate change. Bangladesh 2.0 aspires to build a modern, innovative, and inclusive nation, ensuring progress and prosperity for every individual while preserving the environment for future generations.

Bangladesh Launches New Satelite

Date: May 11, 2018   Time:10:25 AM

Bangladesh marked a significant milestone in its technological journey by launching its first satellite, Bangabandhu-1, on May 11, 2018. The satellite was launched from the Kennedy Space Center in Florida using SpaceX's Falcon 9 rocket. Named after the Father of the Nation, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, this satellite symbolizes Bangladesh’s entry into the global space community.
